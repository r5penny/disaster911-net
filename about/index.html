Credentials & Certifications

Restoration isn't a handyman job — it's a technical profession governed by nationally recognized standards. My certifications aren't just wall decorations.

  • IICRC Certified — Water Restoration Technician (WRT)
    The industry standard for water damage assessment and mitigation. Follows ANSI/IICRC S500.
  • IICRC Certified — Applied Structural Drying (ASD)
    Advanced certification for psychrometric drying of structures. Ensures complete moisture elimination to prevent secondary mold growth.
  • Michigan Residential Builder's License #2101187907

How We Work

Every job follows the same proven process — no shortcuts, no guesswork.

  1. Emergency Dispatch (within minutes of your call) — Ryan answers, assesses severity over the phone, and dispatches the crew immediately.
  2. On-Site Inspection & Documentation — Moisture mapping, thermal imaging, photo documentation. Everything your insurance adjuster will need.
  3. Water Extraction & Drying — Commercial-grade Phoenix air movers and dehumidifiers. We monitor drying progress daily using moisture meters.
  4. Demolition of Unsalvageable Materials — Saturated drywall, flooring, and insulation removed following IICRC S500 protocols.
  5. Mold Prevention Treatment — Antimicrobial application to all affected surfaces.
  6. Insurance Claim Coordination — We work directly with your adjuster, submit documentation, and handle supplements when needed.
  7. Full Rebuild & Restoration — Drywall, paint, flooring, trim — we return your home to pre-loss condition or better.
